About me

I like saying that I'm pretty lucky as feel like I've lived three lives instead of one, but this one is my favourite. I was an engineer of optoelectronic and laser devices and systems in my first life, and then I became a lawyer and worked in this field for more than ten years.


My relocation to London helped me to really develop my English skills, and also gave me the opportunity to try myself in coding. I began learning Web Development in January 2021 and quickly realised that this was precisely what I have tried to find my whole life. I love learning new things, am open-minded to new technologies and tools, and want to create beautiful and functional websites.


I don't like to wish for something, but I always have a detailed plan for self-development. Currently, I'm working on my projects, deepening my knowledge of Web Development and discovering modern tools and features. At the same time, as a real team player and a person who likes communicating, learning and sharing knowledge and experience with others, I'm ready to try myself at more significant projects.

I have been committed to learning throughout my whole life. So, my slogan is "I can't go a single day without picking up something new." My organisational skills also assist me in facilitating learning fast and constantly developing my abilities.


I like to dive deep into the subject I study and use my imagination to visualise and understand the whole process before putting what I've learned into practice.


Although I am a self-taught person and over time have developed ways to solve many problems independently, I am always open to feedback and constructive criticism, and I am happy to discuss and solve problems with my mentors, management and teammates.
